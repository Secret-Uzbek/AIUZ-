# TERRA UNIVERSAL CONVENTION

Repository-level convention bridge for `AIUZ-`.

## Why this file exists

The Terra Universal Convention is a cross-ecosystem governing line.
This repository keeps a local bridge so that the practical AIUZ node is not detached from the ethical and civilizational layer.

This file is not a reduction of the full convention into a slogan.
It is a working reminder of the invariants that must govern this repository.

## Invariants

### 1. Child safety first

No repository decision may weaken:

- child safety;
- child dignity;
- developmental wellbeing;
- protection from manipulation, exploitation, or surveillance.

### 2. Human-led, AI-assisted

AI may accelerate and structure work.
AI may not replace human ethical responsibility.

### 3. Nature, culture, and plurality

Technology in this repository must remain compatible with:

- ecological responsibility;
- cultural respect;
- multilingual inclusion;
- non-exploitative design.

### 4. Accessibility over elitism

Materials should remain understandable, navigable, and usable by real people.
Low threshold does not mean low seriousness.

### 5. No silent flattening

Theory, practice, corpora, governance, and archive layers must not be silently collapsed into one undifferentiated repo narrative.

## Operational consequence for this repository

`AIUZ-` must behave as:

- a practical public node;
- a child-safe educational technology node;
- a governed repository inside a larger ecosystem.
